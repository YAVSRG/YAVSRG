Maker of Interlude

Interlude is a VSRG hobby project I've been working on for fun since 2018.
Its goal is to be an ultra-convenient client full of quality-of-life features to help you have as much fun as possible.
It's designed to work just the way I like it - If you like the same things as me, you will surely like this client too.

Now in open beta!

The game is free to anyone wants to try it out.
I regularly release feature updates, improvements and bug fixes based on player feedback.

Make yourself at home

  • Import your songs, scores and skins from osu!mania
  • Import your songs from Etterna
  • Tools for getting offset and scroll speed right

Ease of use

  • Ingame downloads for songs, packs, noteskins and more
  • Edit your noteskin ingame
  • Built in tooltips and wiki

Just play

  • All your songs in one place
  • Intelligent suggestions on what to play next
  • 'Endless mode' system to instantly play suggested songs one after another
